WELCOME TO BAY WALLET

BAY WALLET is created to assist users in successfully managing their money. Whether your aim is to pay off debt, save for a major purchase, or just have more control over your spending, BAY WALLET offers the information and resources you need to reach your financial objectives.

Functionality


BAY WALLET's features can be customized to whatever budgetting plan you want, things like Budget Creation, Goal Settings, Income and Debt Tracking, these options allow you to organize aspects of everything worth spending or not. In the end, what matters is that functionality is key, and we have given you the entire toolset to do so.

Zero Integration


BAY WALLET doesn't have financial integration, While some might think that this function is necessary, it is only through your choice to find something that allows you to connect with financial institutions, we provide a free system that can plan how to spend money for you, and hiring professionals can be a bit taxing on your wallet.

Analysis


BAY WALLET is also able to give you Financial Insight, giving you detailed visuals of your financial health, you can also be able to compare previous budget plans through the Calendar, as a reference point, you can also Export and Backup your plans to prevent data loss from corruption or device damages. Our simple system allows these functions to work, all that's needed is your input.
